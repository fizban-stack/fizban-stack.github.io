Just Hacking Training (JHT) is a technical education platform founded by John Hammond that provides hands-on cybersecurity instruction at various skill levels.

## Key Features

- Structured courses and learning paths
- Free upskill challenges for skill building
- Hack-alongs with instructor-led demonstrations
- Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions
- Content from industry experts (All-Stars)
- Training for beginners through advanced practitioners

## Why I Recommend It

Just Hacking offers a diverse range of training formats that make cybersecurity education accessible to everyone. The combination of free challenges and premium courses means you can start learning without a financial barrier, while the hack-alongs and CTF competitions provide practical, hands-on experience.
