Hack The Box is a popular online platform offering realistic penetration testing challenges through vulnerable machines, CTF competitions, and advanced labs.

## Key Features

- Realistic vulnerable machines and challenges
- Active and retired machine walkthroughs
- CTF competitions and challenges
- Pro Labs for advanced training
- Certifications (CPTS, CBBH)
- Active community and forums

## Why I Recommend It

Hack The Box offers some of the most realistic penetration testing scenarios available. The machines closely simulate real-world environments, making it excellent preparation for actual security assessments and certifications.
