Intigriti is a European bug bounty platform that offers Hackademy, a free training program designed to help security researchers get started in bug bounty hunting.

## Key Features

- Free bug bounty training (Hackademy)
- European-focused bug bounty programs
- Learning resources and guides
- Community support
- Vulnerability disclosure programs
- Beginner-friendly approach

## Why I Recommend It

Intigriti's Hackademy provides excellent training specifically tailored for bug bounty hunting. The platform is particularly valuable for those interested in European bug bounty programs.
