YesWeHack is a European bug bounty platform that offers YesWeHack Dojo, a training platform with challenges and learning resources for ethical hackers.

## Key Features

- Free training platform (YesWeHack Dojo)
- Bug bounty programs
- Hands-on challenges
- Learning paths
- European market focus
- Community support

## Why I Recommend It

YesWeHack Dojo provides practical training with real-world scenarios. The platform is well-designed for learning bug bounty techniques and offers opportunities to apply skills in actual programs.
