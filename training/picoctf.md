picoCTF is a free computer security game designed for middle and high school students, but suitable for anyone learning cybersecurity. It features year-round challenges and an annual competition.

## Key Features

- Beginner-friendly challenges
- Multiple categories (cryptography, web exploitation, forensics, etc.)
- Year-round practice problems
- Annual competition
- Educational resources and tutorials
- Completely free

## Why I Recommend It

picoCTF is an excellent starting point for beginners. The challenges are well-designed, educational, and cover a wide range of security topics in an accessible format.
