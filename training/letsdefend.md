LetsDefend is a platform focused on defensive cybersecurity training, providing hands-on SOC analyst experience and real-world incident response scenarios.

## Key Features

- SOC analyst simulation
- Incident response scenarios
- SIEM and log analysis training
- Blue team focused content
- Real-world attack simulations
- Free and premium tiers

## Why I Recommend It

LetsDefend is one of the best resources for blue team training. It provides realistic SOC environments and incident response scenarios that are essential for defensive security professionals.
