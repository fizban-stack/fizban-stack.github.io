Simply Cyber provides cybersecurity career guidance, training resources, and community support, particularly focused on helping beginners enter the cybersecurity field.

## Key Features

- Career guidance and mentorship
- Training resources
- Community support
- Beginner-focused content
- Career path recommendations
- Free and premium content

## Why I Recommend It

Simply Cyber is an excellent resource for career guidance in cybersecurity. The platform helps beginners understand career paths and provides resources to help navigate the cybersecurity job market.
