TryHackMe is an online platform that teaches cybersecurity through hands-on virtual labs. It offers guided learning paths, realistic attack scenarios, and a gamified approach to cybersecurity education.

## Key Features

- Interactive virtual machines and labs
- Guided learning paths for beginners to advanced
- Real-world attack scenarios and simulations
- Community-driven content and challenges
- Progress tracking and achievements
- Both free and premium content available

## Why I Recommend It

TryHackMe provides an excellent entry point for cybersecurity learning with its structured paths and beginner-friendly approach. The hands-on labs reinforce theoretical knowledge and the platform makes learning engaging and accessible.
