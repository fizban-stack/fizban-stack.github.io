The Homelab Show covered everything about building and maintaining a homelab. While it has stopped producing new episodes, the archive contains valuable content for homelab enthusiasts.

## Key Features

- Homelab building guides
- Infrastructure discussions
- Hardware and software reviews
- Homelab maintenance
- Project ideas
- Community content

## Why I Recommend It

Much like the Self-Hosted podcast, The Homelab Show helped inspire my homelab journey. The archived episodes provide excellent guidance on building and maintaining a homelab, which is essential for hands-on cybersecurity learning.
