What the Hack? is an award-winning true cybercrime podcast that tells memorable stories about cybercrime, scams, and digital threats while providing practical advice on staying safe online. The podcast features interviews with cybersecurity experts, digital forensics professionals, and former cybercriminals to explore the human side of cybercrime and how to protect yourself.

## Key Features

- Award-winning true cybercrime storytelling
- Memorable stories from the world of cybercrime
- Practical advice on staying safe online
- Interviews with cybersecurity experts and digital forensics professionals
- Coverage of scams, fraud, AI security threats, and digital forensics
- Real-world cybercrime case studies
- Regular episodes with current threats and trends

## Why I Recommend It

What the Hack? combines engaging storytelling with practical cybersecurity advice, making it both entertaining and educational. The award-winning format brings real cybercrime stories to life while providing actionable advice you can use to protect yourself. The podcast covers everything from sextortion scams and deed fraud to AI-powered threats and digital forensics, making it valuable for understanding both the threats we face and how to defend against them.
