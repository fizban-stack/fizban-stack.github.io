Self-Hosted was a podcast about self-hosting, privacy, and taking control of your data. While it has stopped producing new episodes, the archive contains excellent content on self-hosting and privacy.

## Key Features

- Self-hosting topics
- Privacy discussions
- Data control and ownership
- Homelab content
- Self-hosting tutorials
- Privacy-focused approach

## Why I Recommend It

Self-Hosted was one of the podcasts that inspired my homelab journey. Even though it's no longer active, the archived episodes provide excellent guidance on self-hosting, privacy, and taking control of your digital life.
