The Hacker's Cache is hosted by Kyser Clark and focuses on offensive cybersecurity and ethical hacking. Every week, the show invites listeners into the world of ethical hacking by interviewing leading offensive security practitioners, including penetration testers, bug bounty hunters, red teamers, and blue teamers who want to better understand the modern hacker mindset.

## Key Features

- Hosted by Kyser Clark
- Weekly interviews with offensive security practitioners
- Focus on ethical hacking and penetration testing
- Career guidance for cybersecurity professionals
- Coverage of tools like Metasploit and certifications like OSCP
- Insights into the modern hacker mindset
- Content for both beginners and experienced professionals
- Regular episodes since 2024

## Why I Recommend It

The Hacker's Cache provides valuable insights into offensive cybersecurity through interviews with leading practitioners. Whether you're a penetration tester, bug bounty hunter, red teamer, or blue teamer looking to understand the attacker mindset, this podcast offers practical knowledge and career guidance. The focus on real-world offensive security makes it valuable for anyone wanting to understand how modern hackers think and operate.
