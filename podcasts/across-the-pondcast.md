Across the Pondcast is a UK-based cybersecurity podcast that covers security news, trends, and developments from a European perspective.

## Key Features

- UK and European cybersecurity focus
- Security news coverage
- Industry trends
- European market perspective
- Regular episodes
- Regional insights

## Why I Recommend It

Across the Pondcast provides valuable perspective from the UK and European cybersecurity scene. The regional focus offers insights that complement US-centric security content.
