Behind the Binary is hosted by Josh Stroschein, a reverse engineer with the FLARE team at Google. The podcast goes beyond the code to share the stories, motivations, and unique perspectives of individuals who dedicate their lives to unraveling the complexities of technology. Episodes explore journeys into the field, the challenges reverse engineers face, and the impact their work has on securing our digital world.

## Key Features

- Hosted by Google FLARE team member Josh Stroschein
- Interviews with reverse engineering experts
- Technical deep dives into malware analysis and tools
- Human stories and career journeys in reverse engineering
- Coverage of topics like Windows internals, EDRs, obfuscation, and FLARE-On competitions
- Insights into the art and science of reverse engineering

## Why I Recommend It

Behind the Binary offers a unique perspective on reverse engineering by combining technical depth with human stories. Hosted by someone from Google's FLARE team, the podcast provides access to expert insights on topics ranging from Windows kernel design and EDRs to malware obfuscation and threat intelligence. The focus on both the technical and human aspects of reverse engineering makes it valuable for malware analysts, security researchers, and anyone curious about this fascinating field.
