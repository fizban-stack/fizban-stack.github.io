DEF CON is the "hacker's conference." Unlike Black Hat, which is corporate, DEF CON is gritty, experimental, and covers niche topics like biohacking, lockpicking, and social engineering.

## Channel Focus

### Unconventional Warfare
- **Social Engineering**: The human element of security (vishing, tailgating).
- **Physical Security**: Lockpicking, elevator hacking, and bypassing access controls.

### The Hacker Mindset
- **Villages**: Talks from specific sub-groups like the Biohacking Village, AI Village, and Voting Village.

## Why Follow This Channel
To understand the creative, chaotic, and unrestricted side of hacking.
