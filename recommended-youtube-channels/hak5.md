## Channel Focus

### Physical Security
- **Implants**: Using malicious USBs, cables, and LAN turtles to gain network access.
- **Social Engineering**: Delivering payloads via physical access.

### Wireless Hacking
- **WiFi**: Protocol analysis, man-in-the-middle attacks, and rogue access points.
- **SDR**: Software Defined Radio and signal analysis.

## Why Follow This Channel
To understand the danger of plugging in unknown devices and how hardware attacks are executed.
