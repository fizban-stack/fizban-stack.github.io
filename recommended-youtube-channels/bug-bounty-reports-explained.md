## Channel Focus

### Report Analysis
- **Reconstruction**: Recreating the steps attackers took to find critical bugs.
- **Impact Analysis**: Understanding why a bug was rated Critical vs. Medium.

### Logic Flaws
- **Business Logic**: Examples of bugs that scanners miss, such as price manipulation or privilege bypasses.
- **Race Conditions**: Real-world examples of exploiting concurrency issues.

## Why Follow This Channel
Reading reports is the best way to learn, and this channel digests them into actionable lessons.
