Audiobookshelf is a self-hosted audiobook and podcast server that gives you complete control over your audio library with seamless sync across devices.

## Key Features

- Audiobook library organization
- Podcast management and streaming
- Progress tracking across devices
- Mobile apps for iOS and Android
- Metadata management
- Multi-user support

## Why I Use It

Audiobookshelf gives me complete control over my audiobook collection. I can listen to my library from any device while tracking progress seamlessly across all of them.
