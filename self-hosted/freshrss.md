FreshRSS is a self-hosted RSS and Atom feed aggregator that gives you control over how you consume content from your favorite websites.

## Key Features

- RSS and Atom feed support
- Article filtering and tagging
- Keyboard shortcuts
- Mobile-friendly interface
- Multi-user support
- API for mobile apps

## Why I Use It

FreshRSS keeps me updated on blogs, news, and content without relying on algorithms or third-party services. I control what I read and how I organize it.
