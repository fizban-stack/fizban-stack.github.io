Home Assistant is a powerful open-source home automation platform that puts local control and privacy first.

## Key Features

- Device and service integration
- Automation and scripting
- Voice control support
- Mobile app for iOS and Android
- Energy monitoring
- Customizable dashboards

## Why I Use It

Home Assistant centralizes control of all my smart home devices with local processing and no cloud dependencies. The automation capabilities are incredibly powerful.
