NoteDiscovery is an intelligent note-taking and organization platform that helps you manage your knowledge with smart linking and discovery features.

## Key Features

- Smart note organization
- Full-text search
- Tag-based categorization
- Markdown support
- Link discovery
- Knowledge graph visualization

## Why I Use It

NoteDiscovery is where I track things I come across that do not fit anywhere else — interesting tools, techniques, findings from research, things I want to revisit. The linking and search features mean I can surface connections between notes that I might not have thought to look for, which is useful when working on something and trying to remember a relevant piece of information I had filed away. For technical research and cybersecurity learning, having a place to collect and connect knowledge beats keeping scattered bookmarks or browser tabs.
