BentoPDF (Stirling-PDF) is a locally hosted web application that provides a comprehensive suite of PDF manipulation tools.

## Key Features

- PDF merging and splitting
- Page rotation and organization
- Image to PDF conversion
- PDF compression
- Watermarking
- OCR support

## Why I Use It

PDFs have a way of making simple tasks unnecessarily painful, and that comes up constantly between work and school. Whether it's splitting out specific pages from a long document, merging several files into one submission, compressing something that's too large to attach to an email, or converting scanned images to a proper PDF, BentoPDF covers it all without making me think twice. The big thing for me is that work and school documents often contain information I'd rather not hand off to some random online converter — keeping everything local means I can handle whatever I need quickly and privately.
