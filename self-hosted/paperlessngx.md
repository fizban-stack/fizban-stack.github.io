Paperless-ngx is a document management system that transforms your physical documents into a searchable online archive with powerful organization features.

## Key Features

- OCR document scanning
- Automatic tagging and organization
- Full-text search
- Email import
- Mobile app support
- Workflow automation

## Why I Use It

Paperless-ngx digitizes and organizes all my documents. The OCR and automatic tagging make it easy to find any document instantly while going paperless.
