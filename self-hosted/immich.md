Immich is a high-performance self-hosted photo and video backup solution that provides a modern alternative to Google Photos.

## Key Features

- Automatic photo backup
- Machine learning for face detection
- Mobile apps for iOS and Android
- Timeline and album organization
- Shared albums
- Video support

## Why I Use It

Immich is my Google Photos replacement. It automatically backs up photos from my phone while keeping everything private and under my control.
