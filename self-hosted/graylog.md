Graylog is a powerful log management platform that centralizes logs from all your servers and applications for easy searching, analysis, and alerting.

## Key Features

- Log aggregation and parsing
- Full-text search
- Real-time alerting
- Dashboard creation
- Stream processing
- Archive and compliance

## Why I Use It

Graylog centralizes logs from all my servers and applications, making troubleshooting and monitoring much easier. The powerful search and alerting features help me catch issues quickly.
