## Adversary Naming Convention

CrowdStrike's distinctive animal-themed naming system: **BEAR** (Russian), **PANDA** (Chinese), **KITTEN** (Iranian), **CHOLLIMA** (North Korean), **SPIDER** (eCrime), **JACKAL** (Hacktivists), and others for different origins. This system enables clear adversary identification, evolution tracking, and campaign linkage.
