Associated with the popular Critical Thinking podcast, HackerNotes provides the "soft skills" and rigorous methodologies required to transition from a hobbyist to a professional bug hunter.

## Core Philosophical Themes

### The Business of Hunting
- **Project Management**: How to track long-term research on a single target.
- **Burnout Prevention**: Balancing high-intensity hunting with sustainable habits.
- **Negotiation**: Best practices for communicating with triage and security teams.

### Technical Strategy
- **Recon Automation**: Building custom pipelines that don't just "spam" tools.
- **Deep Diving**: Moving past low-hanging fruit to find architectural flaws.
- **Target Selection**: Criteria for choosing high-yield programs in 2026.
