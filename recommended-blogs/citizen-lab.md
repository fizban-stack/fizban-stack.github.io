The Citizen Lab is the world leader in identifying and documenting the use of commercial spyware against non-combatants.

## Impact Research
- **Spyware Forensics**: Analyzing Pegasus and similar zero-click mobile exploits.
- **Digital Surveillance**: Mapping the infrastructure of global surveillance companies.
- **Policy & Ethics**: Researching the legal and ethical implications of the global spyware trade.

## Why Follow This Blog

The Citizen Lab occupies a unique position at the intersection of technical security research, human rights advocacy, and policy work. Their investigations into commercial spyware have exposed some of the most sophisticated exploitation techniques in the world while simultaneously protecting journalists, activists, and civil society from state-sponsored surveillance.
